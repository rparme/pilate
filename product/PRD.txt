Website Requirements — Executive Mobility & Core Program (NYC)
1. Objective
Create a minimal, premium landing page to validate demand for a private mobility & core strength coaching program targeting:

Men 35–65

Tech & finance professionals

NYC-based (online + optional in-person sessions)

Primary goal:
Drive qualified applicants to complete an intake form.

No e-commerce. No complex infrastructure. Single conversion goal.

2. Positioning
This is NOT a general Pilates site.

Tone must be:

Professional

Structured

Performance-oriented

Calm and credible

Executive-level

Avoid:

Fitness influencer aesthetic

Bright colors

“Yoga flow” tone

Playful language

Overly long copy

3. Target Audience
Men aged 35–65:

Tech professionals

Finance professionals

Founders / leadership roles

Sit 8–12 hours daily

Experiencing stiffness (back, hips, neck)

Interested in long-term strength & mobility

4. Core Offer
6-Week Private Spine & Mobility Reset

Delivery:

Online (Zoom)

Optional in-person sessions in NYC

Program includes:

Personalized movement assessment

Core strengthening

Hip & spine mobility work

Structured coaching

Schedule-compatible sessions
